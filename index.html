Services

Classic

Individual eyelash extensions applied to each individual natural lash to create fuller, thicker looking lashes. Great for those who have full natural lashes. All lash services may take up to 4 hours depending on fullness of natural lashes.

Hybrid

A 50/50 mix of classic and volume eyelash extensions applied to the natural lash to create a natural glam look. Want the best of both worlds or not sure if a classic or volume set is right for you? The Hybrid set is perfect! Great for those with full or sparse natural lashes. All lash services may take up to 4 hours depending on fullness of natural lashes.

Volume

Light and fluffy volume eyelash extensions applied to the natural lash to create the ultimate voluminous look. Love a dramatic look or have a damaged lash line? The Volume set will disguise any imperfections to the lash line by filling lash gaps and will give you the perfect amount of glamour and drama. Great for those with thin or sparse natural lashes. All lash services may take up to 4 hours depending on fullness of natural lashes.
